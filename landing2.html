Overcoming Challenges in French Rail Through Digitilization

A Consulting Style Report Analyzing the Applications of Industry 4.0 Technologies on French Rail Companies

Awarded USC's Global Scholar Distinction for this Report

This research report recommended French transportation companies to utilize Industry 4.0 technologies, such as The Internet of Things and cyber-physical systems, to overcome recent billion-dollar losses from labor strikes, COVID-19, and infrastructure renovations required by the 2019 European Green Deal. The report was reviewed by a USC faculty committee alongside an additional essay, so I could earn Global Scholar Distinction, an honor fewer than 50 seniors receive each year.
